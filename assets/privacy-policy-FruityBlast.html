Privacy Policy for Fruity Blast

About Fruity Blast

Fruity Blast is an engaging match-3 puzzle game featuring colorful graphics, exciting levels, and challenging gameplay. Players progress through a map-based level system, earning stars and unlocking new content as they match their way through various puzzles with vibrant animations and effects.

This application is developed and maintained by dlgn.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data collection and usage in our Fruity Blast game.

Information We Collect

Local Game Data

  • Game progress and level completion data
  • Stars earned and in-game achievements
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Local high scores and gameplay statistics

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain core game functionality
  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To enable necessary game features like level progression

Data Storage and Security

  • Your game data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload or store your personal game data on remote servers
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect any local data

Third-Party Services

  • We do not share your personal game data with any third parties

Device Permissions

Fruity Blast requires only minimal permissions necessary for gameplay:

  • Storage access: To save game progress locally

Children's Privacy

Fruity Blast is designed to be appropriate for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game collects no personal data from any users, regardless of age.
