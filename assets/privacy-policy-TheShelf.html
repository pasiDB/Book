Privacy Policy for The Shelf

About The Shelf

The Shelf is a modern book reading app that provides access to a vast collection of free public domain books. Users can browse, search, and read books, manage their reading progress, and customize their reading experience with various settings.

This application is developed and maintained by dlgn.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data collection and usage in The Shelf app.

Information We Collect

Local App Data

  • Reading progress and bookmarks
  • Downloaded and cached books
  • App settings and preferences
  • Search history (stored locally)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain core reading functionality
  • To save your reading progress and preferences locally on your device
  • To enable features like offline reading and personalized settings

Data Storage and Security

  • Your app data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload or store your personal app data on remote servers
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect any local data

Third-Party Services

  • The Shelf does not share your personal app data with any third parties
  • Book metadata and content are sourced from public APIs, primarily Gutendex (Project Gutenberg)

How We Ensure Only Free Books Are Available

The Shelf is committed to providing only books that are free of copyright restrictions and available in the public domain. We use the following measures to ensure this:

  • We source book data from Gutendex, which provides metadata from Project Gutenberg, a trusted repository of public domain books.
  • When querying the API, we explicitly filter for books marked as public domain by using the copyright=false parameter. This ensures that only books with confirmed public domain status are shown in the app.
  • We do not display or allow access to books with unknown or restricted copyright status.
  • We periodically review our data sources and update our filters to maintain compliance with copyright laws and app store policies.

Device Permissions

The Shelf requires only minimal permissions necessary for app functionality:

  • Storage access: To save downloaded books and app data locally
  • Internet access: To fetch book metadata and content from public sources

Children's Privacy

The Shelf is designed to be appropriate for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app collects no personal data from any users, regardless of age.

Your Rights

As a user of The Shelf, you have the following rights:

  • Access: View what data is stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: Delete your app data from the app at any time by using the Clear Data feature or clearing app data through your device settings
  • Control: Manage app permissions through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • Significant changes will be notified to you through the app or via the app store
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically

Consent
